Our Story

Built by someone who didn't know the rules either.

I'm the first person in my family to go to college. I remember sitting in my car before my first real job interview, not knowing what "behavioral questions" meant, having no one to call.

I got the job eventually — but it took three failed interviews to learn what I should have known going in. Nobody should have to fail their way through a process that could have been explained in an afternoon.

FirstRound is the coach I wish I had. It doesn't assume you know the rules. It explains them, practices with you, and sends you in ready.
